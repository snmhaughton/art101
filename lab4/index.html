Task 1: Making Guacamole

  1. Gather ingredients. (3 avocados, 1/2 small onion, 2 Roma tomatoes, 3 tablespoons of cilantro, 1 jalapeno, 2 garlic cloves, 1 lime, 1/2 tablespoon sea salt)
  2. Gather kitchen tools needed to make the guacamole. (Cutting board, knife, one big bowl for mixing)
  3. Slice the three ripe avocados in half, remove the pit and scoop the flesh into a mixing bowl
  4. Use a fork to gently mash them to your desired level of chunky or smooth
  5. Dice the jalapeno, onions, and tomatoes to desired size (small pieces are recommended)
  6. Dice the jalapeno and cilantro into tiny pieces
  7. Cut lime in half
  8. Add the onions, tomatoes, cilantro, jalapeno pepper, garlic, lime juice and salt to the mixing bowl with the avocados and stir everything together
  9. Serve as desired

Above is our list of making guacamole, a step by step guide of the pseudocode format for an everyday task.
