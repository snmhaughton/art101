Watering the Mind

Welcome to Watering the Mind, an interactive mental health game! Choose a low-maintenance houseplant as your avatar, and get ready to learn about day-to-day activities that can improve your mental health. The Covid-19 pandemic has impacted all of our lives this year, and some of us have been spending more time at home than ever before. Feeling cooped up and inactive can have a negative impact on mental health, so we created a game to help! Watering the Mind aims to provide sustainable, everyday acts of self-care that can improve mental health. As a bonus, you will learn facts about your chosen houseplant on the way!
